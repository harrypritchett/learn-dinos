Spinosaurus

These are my top 5 facts about a Spinosaurus

  1. Spinosaurus is known to have eaten fish, and most scientists believe that it hunted both terrestrial and aquatic prey.
  2. Spinosaurus is the longest known terrestrial carnivore.
  3. Spinosaurus (meaning “spine lizard”) is a genus of spinosaurid dinosaur that lived in what now is North Africa.
  4. It lived in a humid environment of tidal flats and mangrove forests alongside many other dinosaurs.
  5. Its skull had a narrow snout filled with straight conical teeth that lacked serrations.
